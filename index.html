What is Sarthak Prayas all about?


Sarthak Prayas, a not for profit organisation under the societies registration act of 1860 based in New Delhi, India, began its journey in 2003 to create a backbone of the patients in the need of blood, especially Thalassemia and create mass awareness about blood donation. We also focus on Child Education for rural and urban areas, creating a platform to change their future and building a positive attitude in them. We have taken a responsibility for Sr. Citizen welfare to give them a better way of living, a space to live in their own conditions with healthiness and happiness. We haven’t forgotten the danger we created to environment hence we work for environment education and awareness.

It was not a mere hobby or an occasional exercise to run the activities on the pattern of several such existing institutions part time basis. It was a mission of creating a human chain of emergency blood donors with valid details, complete verification of the donor as well as of the needy to prevent misuse and effective management systems. We wanted to inculcate positive attitude, good human values in children and give a secure, healthy and happy environment to our elders. We also wanted to develop a sense of awareness about environment and work towards a green environment for our future generations. The whole course required volunteers to aware people and contributes their time and skills. We needed to have people with the right attitude and vision. More than anything else we required support and funds, small and big from individuals, institutions and corporate houses.

We created this dream for Sarthak Prayas and we have never looked back to achieve our goals.

Health

Mission Save Life

Delhi is a city where people come from all parts of India and even from all over the world to get medical treatment and often require blood during their treatment. They usually don’t know anybody in the city, most of the time don’t have access or knowledge about internet resulting they meet the touts to get the blood.

Sarthak Prayas comes to help for these patients by running first of its kind Phone Blood Helpline from 8 AM to 8 PM, 365 days in the year. Patients, their care takers or the Blood Bank officials can call our helpline numbers at +91 92789 57509, 011 45650560 to register the request. They provide the details of the patients and we try to fulfil the need within 2 hours of receiving the call by connecting to the nearest Emergency Blood Donor from that hospital.

This is our dream project and we are committed to fulfill this mission. The idea is to create the largets human chain of EMERGENCY BLOOD DONORS who will always be ready to donate blood in any emergency. Our helpline numbers are with various hospitals in order to give timely help to the patient if they don’t have stock of a particular group.

Every month we receive approximate 150 calls for all the blood groups from all over Delhi/NCR. Our dream is to create a chain of minimum 500000 such people in Delhi/NCR and in other parts of India so that nobody will die just because of shortage of blood. But, due to shortage of funds and high costing involves, facing a slow growth in the mission. Our team is continually striving to achieve this dream which will reduce untimely death significantly.

Remedial Education Centres:-

In these remedial education centers we teach those students who are already a part of govt. school. We give these students life education, teach them how to deal with real life situations and also help them with their homework given in their school.

We have 8 such centers running in Delhi/NCR where we have approx. 40 students studying in each center. These children are given packed food every day after the class and also we celebrate children’s Birthday every month where we make them cut cake and distribute gifts amongst them.

Following activities are conducted in our Remedial Education Centres:

  • Apart from basic education, 90 minutes coaching focuses on their Personality Development, Spoken English, Health and Hygiene, Life and Soft Skills and Positive Attitude
  • Daily packed food (Biscuit, Baked Chips, Fruit) is given
  • Monthly Birthday Celebration
  • Regular Medical Checkup
  • Quarterly Educational Outings
  • Monthly Books and Stationary distribution

Elders

Second Innings Home

We live in a superfast world where we run around to achieve our goals and set new targets, trying too hard to be in the rat race, want to achieve luxury and be happy in this monetary world. But, in this race, somewhere we have forgotten to spend time with our family, our elders having rich experience of life and ready to share with the young minds which will eventually help them to achieve success and happiness easily. Ironically, our elders are not so blessed to share the golden experience with their young ones. They are left alone at home or roads and they have no one to talk to, forced to go out and spend time in parks, outside religious places, under the flyovers. Their needs are simple yet difficult to achieve. Sarthak Prayas has taken this responsibility to give these people in their golden age a Secure and Happy environment to spend their lifetime with the same age group and likeminded people

With your continued support we have finally started our Second Inning Home since 15th of August 2015 . It is a unique old age home for destitute, poor and abandoned Sr. Citizens in Chatterpur, Delhi. Your efforts to strengthen our endeavours have been delightful as We already received your contribution on certain items and few we have bought on your behalf to start the operations.

Adopt a Senior

After working hard and struggling throughout for food, shelter, family, and various other things when one starts reaching an age where body and soul doesn’t allow to work hard and under less privileged conditions things become even tougher. Sarthak Prayas family sensed that the capital city Delhi is most unsafe for elderly and hence came up with the idea of making their lives a little better with our ability and limited resources.

Sarthak Prayas visited the slums and identified old age people who were in the worst condition amongst the lot and where also not in the situation to work and earn bread for themselves on regular bases. Monthly ration as well as quarterly clothing and toiletries is provided to them. These Sr. Citizens get together in a local area nearby their slums every month and we distribute the stuff to them which includes things like rice, flour, bathing soap, washing soap, and all other things.

Following Items are distributed every month to our adopted Sr. Citizens:

  • 5 kg Wheat.
  • 2 kg Rice.
  • Half kg 4 different types of pulses.
  • 250 gms Tea Leaves.
  • Bathing and Washing soap 1 each.
  • 1 kg Sugar.
  • 1 Kg Salt.
  • 100 gm Garam Masala
  • 100 gm Red Chilli Powder
  • 100 gm Corriander Powder
  • 100 gm Jeera
  • 100 gm Turmeric Powder
  • Lt Cooking Oil
  • 1 Big Pack of Biscuit

Environment

Green India, Clean India

This movement was initiated in 2010 at Sarthak Prayas as there was a lot of buzz around then about pollution control and global warming, but very little work has been done. We took this initiative to create green force against Global Warming and a want to make them Ambassadors to spread awareness for Green India Clean India.

Sarthak Prayas is actively involved in organising awareness camps at public places and train students about the dangers of Global Warming to ensure our future generations live in a healthier and happier environment. We took this initiative of and trained 1000 school students about global warming and its negative effects and things that can be done and should be taken care of to prevent global warming. We planted 1000 saplings and made these school children Green Ambassadors and intern these children adopted these saplings and are looking after them.
